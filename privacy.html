Privacy Policy for TripWise

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the TripWise mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be collected through the App.

2. Information Collection and Use

TripWise uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you and your device’s performance.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or phone number) provided by the user directly.
  • Usage and Technical Data: To improve the App's stability and user experience, we collect technical data such as crash logs, device identifiers, and app performance metrics through third-party services.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services provided by Google (Firebase):

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used to understand app usage and interactions.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to identify and fix app crashes.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: Used to monitor app speed and responsiveness.

These services collect data in accordance with their own Privacy Policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

User-created data (e.g., trip checklists) is stored locally on your device. Technical logs and analytics are transmitted securely via HTTPS to Google’s servers.

5. Data Deletion Request

Users can request the deletion of their collected technical data (Analytics and Crashlytics identifiers). To submit a request, please contact us at: max.app.engineering@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: max.app.engineering@gmail.com
